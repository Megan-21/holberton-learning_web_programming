About Me

I am Megan and I am a junior. I like to play several instruments such as piano, guitar, and violin. I am interested in math and science. I currently want to study computer science engineering in college. I also like to volunteer at Chabot Space and Science Museum.

I go to Carondelet High School located in Concord, California. Carondelet High School is an all-girls, private, Catholic High School. Carondelet is full of energetic, positive students. The faculty and staff are always friendly and available to talk.

I created this website so that people could be aware of the endangerment of whales and how they could help the effort in saving them. I think that it is important to try and save endangered animals from becoming extinct. Often, the reason why these animals are labeled endangered or critically endangered is because of the actions of human beings. I also created this website so that I could spread the awareness of certain endangered animals and how others can save them.

During the summer of 2019, I learned how to code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Bash, and Vagrant at Holberton School in San Francisco, California. Holberton School offers a two week summer camp in which students learn about the basics of programming and web design.

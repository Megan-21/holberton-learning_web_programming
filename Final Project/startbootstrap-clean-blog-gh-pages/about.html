About Me

This is what I do.

I am Megan and I am a junior. I like to play several instruments such as piano, guitar, and violin. I also like to volunteer at Chabot Space and Science Museum.

I go to Carondelet High School located in Concord, California. Carondelet High School is an all-girls, private, Catholic High School. Carondelet is full of energetic, positive students. The faculty and staff are always friendly and available to talk.

I created this website so that people could be aware of the endangerment of whales and how they could help the effort in saving endangered creatures.

During the summer of 2019, I learned how to code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at Holberton School in San Francisco, California. Holberton School offers a two week summer camp in which students learn about the basics of programming and web design.

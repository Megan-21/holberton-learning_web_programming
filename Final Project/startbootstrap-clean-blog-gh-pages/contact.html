How to Help

The Current Effort

Many organizations are working on increasing the number of whales currently on the earth as well as conserving the ones that currently live such as the World Wide Fund for Nature and Defenders of Wildlife. For example, the WWF teaches local residents of the Philippines how to help stranded whales, dolphins, and porpoises. The Defenders' have been persuading Congress to enact the SAVE Right Whales Act. In January 2018, they also filed suit against the National Marine Fisheries Service for violating serverate acts.

Some Ways to Help

  • Reduce the amount of waste you create.
  • Try to purchase local goods to avoid excessive shipping. Often, ship strikes fatally harm the whales.
  • Do you part in an effort to reduce climate change
  • Walk, take public transportation, or bike. Attempt to use an vehicle that runs on gas and oil. Gas and oil development produce noise that can cause the whales to lose their hearing.
  • Sign local petitions.
